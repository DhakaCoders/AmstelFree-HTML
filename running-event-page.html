KALLITHEA HALF MARATHON 2020

Saturday, 21. March 2020 - Sunday, 22. March 2020
Kallithea, Athens

Nireas Triathlon is the primary and longest established triathlon race organiser in Cyprus. It is a registered company and complies with all regulations and taxes of the Cyprus Republic.
Nireas Triathlon started 24 years ago with a small group of passionate athletes who set out with the aim of making Nireas the first point of reference for the sport in Cyprus.

Our mission is to organise high quality events in order to expand triathlon locally but also to build Cyprus as
a first-choice destination for overseas athletes. Nireas Triathlon now offers a series of quality races attracting hundreds of athletes including some of the sport’s top names. Every Nireas race is an exciting new challenge.

Location

Λεωφ. Δενδροποτάμου & Ελ. Βενιζέλου.
